**📝 STT生テキスト**: [詳細を見る](#stt-af2a3b1b)

The speaker explains that Christ’s death has fully paid for sin, making forgiveness real, complete, and available to all who believe, but warns that this truth is often misunderstood. The speaker distinguishes between a Christian who still struggles with sin and the false idea of a Christian who never sins. They emphasize that Jesus had to die to deal with sin, and therefore sin is never something with which Christians should make peace.
